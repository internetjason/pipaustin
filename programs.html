Programs

PIP Austin is proud to offer two programs at this time for new moms and dads.

Newborn PIPsqueaks Groups: 0-4 months {$150}

Our newborn groups bring together parents with babies 0-4 months old who all live in the same area of Austin. The 12-week program is led by a trained facilitator who will help you traverse the joys and challenges of being new parents. These sessions meet in the group members’ homes. Parents choose from a list of more than 35 parenting topics to discuss during their sessions. The registration fee comes out to roughly $12.50 a week, and includes access to a list of guest speakers that can be brought in for any of the topics that are covered during the 12 weeks, offers from our sponsors that are exclusive to PIP participants, and of course a network of families in your area with whom you will get to grow, learn and play alongside. The daytime sessions are for one parent and his/her baby, and the evening sessions are geared toward couples and their babies. All first-time parents are encouraged to join!

Baby PIPsqueaks Groups: 5-10 months {$100}

Our baby groups bring together parents with babies 5-10 months old who all live in the same area of Austin. The 8-week program is led by a trained facilitator who will help you traverse the joys and challenges of being new parents. These sessions meet in the group members’ homes. Parents choose from a list of more than 30 parenting topics to discuss during their sessions. The registration fee comes out to roughly $12.50 a week, and includes access to a list of guest speakers that can be brought in for any of the topics that are covered during the 8 weeks, offers from our sponsors that are exclusive to PIP participants, and of course a network of families in your area with whom you will get to grow, learn and play alongside. The daytime sessions are for one parent and his/her baby, and the evening sessions are geared toward couples and their babies. All first-time parents are encouraged to join!

Coming Soon

Partners in Parenting is continually working to provide programming to cover families in all stages of parenthood and walks of life. Whether you are first-time parents or are welcoming your second or third child, PIP has plans for just about everyone. Please like us on Facebook and check back often as we update our selection of programs and groups.

Once you have registered for a program, we will email you all the information you will need, including your facilitator’s name, the location of the initial meeting, and recommendations for materials to bring to your first gathering.

If you have any further questions about our current program offerings, please do not hesitate to contact Carolyn at carolyn@pipaustin.org.
